About WellPredict

Welcome to WellPredict, your trusted platform for symptom-based disease prediction. Our goal is to empower individuals with insights into their health based on reported symptoms, fostering early awareness and informed decisions.

How It Works

WellPredict employs state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms—Naive Bayes, Decision Tree, Random Forest, and K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)—to analyze symptoms and deliver accurate disease predictions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make healthcare more accessible and personalized through innovative technologies. We strive to contribute to early disease detection and prevention, promoting a healthier society.
