About WellPredict

Welcome to WellPredict, your trusted platform for symptom-based disease prediction. Our goal is to empower individuals with insights into their health based on reported symptoms, fostering early awareness and informed decisions.


How It Works

WellPredict employs state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms—Naive Bayes, Decision Tree, Random Forest, and K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)—to analyze symptoms and deliver accurate disease predictions.


Dataset

Our dataset consists of information on 131 symptoms and 41 diseases. Each row in the dataset represents a patient case with reported symptoms and the corresponding diagnosed disease. The dataset is used to train our machine learning models to predict diseases based on reported symptoms.


Machine Learning Models

We use four different machine learning models for disease prediction:

  • Naive Bayes: A probabilistic classifier based on Bayes' theorem with strong independence assumptions between features.
  • Decision Tree: A tree-like model where an internal node represents a feature, the branch represents a decision rule, and each leaf node represents an outcome.
  • Random Forest: An ensemble learning method that constructs multiple decision trees during training and outputs the mode of the classes (classification) or mean prediction (regression) of the individual trees.
  • K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN): A non-parametric method used for classification and regression that works by finding the K nearest data points in the training set to a given input point.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make healthcare more accessible and personalized through innovative technologies. We strive to contribute to early disease detection and prevention, promoting a healthier society.
